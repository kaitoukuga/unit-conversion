Unit Conversions

Temperature



To convert celsuis to fahrenheit yourself, use this formula replacing the `C` with your temperature in celsuis: (C x 9/5) + 32
Weight



To convert kilograms to pounds yourself, use this formula replacing the `kg` with your weight in kilogram: kg x 2.205
Distance



To convert kilometers to miles yourself, use this formula replacing the `km` with your distance in kilometer: km ÷ 1.609
